About Me

Tessa Bennion is an academic painter working primarily in oil, charcoal, and graphite. Her practice balances direct observation with long-form studio work.

She draws inspiration from classical ateliers, museum study, and the rhythms of everyday life. Portraits, still life arrangements, and plein air studies are forms of expressing her soul and the moments that capture her attention.

When not at the easel, Tessa is either enjoying the mountains in the State of Alaska or somewhere in Anchorage singing jazz/funk fusion for her band the Work Shoes. This site is a living catalog of finished works, process notes, and the drawings that knit it all together.
